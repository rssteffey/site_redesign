About Shawn

Shawn makes things!


Currently during the day, those things are Javascript-based applications, with a smattering of Java, API design, and various other backend things.


In his free time, those things are more varied, built with a smattering of whatever tools were needed for the project at hand. Recent projects include a multiplayer video game for party nights, a slot machine designed with Unity and built from scraps of an old desk, and a series of art prints inspired by the sea and Japanese wood block printing.


Located just south of Nashville, TN, he lives with his wife, dog, and looming omnipresent digital oversight. (Alexandria, Echo, and Alexa - respectively)
